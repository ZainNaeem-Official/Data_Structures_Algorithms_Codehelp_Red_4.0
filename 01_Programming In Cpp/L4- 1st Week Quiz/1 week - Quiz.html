Question 15
Which loop is guaranteed to execute at least once?
A. for
B. while
C. do-while ‚úì
D. None
Explanation: The do-while loop executes the body first and checks the condition afterward, ensuring it runs at least once. Both for and while loops check the condition before execution, so they may not run at all if the condition is initially false.
